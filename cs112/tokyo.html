Here's Our Top Travel Picks For Tokyo, Japan

TOP ACTIVITIES

  • Tokyo Skytree

    • Tokyo Skytree is a sky scrapper in Tokyo. The second talklest structure in the world. You can go to the top and see a 360 degree view of the city below from over 2000ft from the air. Theres also a restraunt.

  • Tokyo Disneyland

    • Some say the best Disneyland Park in the world. Tokyo Disney Sea a undersea themed Disneyland with a center Volcano in the ceneter. Unique rides and experiences you can only get at this Disney park. A must for any Disney lover

  • Akihabara

    • Akihabara is a buzzing shopping hub famed for its electronics retailers, ranging from tiny stalls to vast department stores like Yodobashi Multimedia Akiba. Venues specializing in manga, anime, and video games include Tokyo Anime Center, for exhibits and souvenirs, and Radio Kaikan with 10 floors of toys, trading cards, and collectibles. Staff dressed as maids or butlers serve tea and desserts at nearby maid cafes.
