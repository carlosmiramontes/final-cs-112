Good Eats

  • Afternoon Tea at Fortnum & Mason

    • Afternoon Tea at Fortnum's is, without doubt, Londons most delicious tradition. Serving extraordinary tea, freshly-baked cakes, still-warm scones and an array finger sandwiches. The tradition of Afternoon Tea dates back more than 200 years when, struck by hunger pangs somewhere between lunch and dinner, the Duchess of Bedford invited her friends over for tea and ‘a light refreshment’ in her rooms at Woburn Abbey. Word of her teatime innovation spread, and on her return to London, ‘Afternoon Tea’ was deliciously inescapable.
